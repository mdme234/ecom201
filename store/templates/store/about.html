About Us

Welcome to Ecom201, your number one source for all products, ie: shoes, bags, t-shirts etc. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of product, with a focus on three characteristics, ie: dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2022 by Abundance Kioko, Ecom201 has come a long way from its beginnings in a starting location, ie: home office, Thika, Kiambu. When the team first started out, our passion for helping everyone be more eco-friendly, providing the best equipment for our fellow students drove us to action, and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the nation, mostly Nairobi area, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the fashion industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely, Abundance Kioko, Founder.
